Case Study / 01

twyne.io

Developed by Ifficient, Twyne is a robust software for lead management, optimization, and distribution, focused on monetization and real-time analytics. Targeted at the B2B market, it serves a few clients in volume, but with high ticket value and demanding customization.

  • Industry AdTech
  • Product Software as a Service
  • Contributions Product Design / Design System / UI Engineering

Goal

Tackling one of the biggest challenges in digital design: creating a modular, scalable, and adaptable interface for highly complex software, with constant growth
in features and requirements.

The visual approach needed to balance performance, readability, and continuous evolution, without requiring a restart for every new feature. Delivering clarity and control even in dense flows.

Proposal

A robust, strategic, and flexible UI kit.
Simplified UI that puts information up front.
Built to last, scale, and ease development.

Each component carries decisions guided by performance, readability, and functional clarity, with special attention to business logic and the journey of corporate users.

Impact

  • Reduced rework for the engineering team.
  • Faster onboarding for new users.
  • A foundation that supported new modules with minimal friction.
  • Strengthened product identity with a modern UI.

Built on solid principles.
Ready for any platform.

Final thoughts

The modularity of the component library enables smart reuse, reducing complexity and increasing development speed.

Decisions on layout, typography, contrast, and spacing were calibrated to support dense data environments without compromising readability.

The result is an interface that adapts to the usage context without losing visual coherence or functional integrity.
